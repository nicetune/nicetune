NiceTune

advanced music search and discovery

About NiceTune.

The goal of NiceTune project is to expose music search and discovery functionality similar to what powers internet radios like Spotify or Pandora to a wide audience of music fans. We are aiming to give users more control of how search criteria is specified and provide a huge variety of search parameters to use. In simple words, tell NiceTune what's your favorite track and what you like about it be it as simple as singer's voice or guitar tone or as complicated as syncopation and it will find you tracks with similar characteristics. Isn't it cool?

How it works.

  • Lookup your favorite track in NiceTune's library.
  • Tell NiceTune what is that you love about the track.
  • Submit your request and enjoy results NiceTune provides you.

Simple, isn't it?
